How It Works

1) Learn

We guide you in understanding the processes, technologies, and mindsets that lead a project to success.

2) Do

Apply the skills and techniques you learn in weekly "to-dos" that are tailored to fit your schedule.

3) Contribute

After completing your "to-do", your work is integrated into the project. Take your learnings and roadblocks to the weekly team meeting to discuss them and push the project forward.
