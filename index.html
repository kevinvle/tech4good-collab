How It Works

1. Learn

We guide you in understanding the processes, technologies, and mindsets that lead a project to success.

2. Do

Apply the skills and techniques you learn in weekly "to-dos" that are tailored to fit your schedule.

3. Contribute

After completing your "to-do", your work is integrated into the project. Take your learnings and roadblocks to the weekly team meeting to discuss them and push the project forward.

  • How much time does it take?

    The minimum time necessary to join the collaboratory is 5 hours per week. This commitment is the minimum that allows us to provide you with the experience we want to share. This time is made up of at least 3 hours a week to provide enough time to accomplish your "to-do", and 2 hours a week for team meetings at one of the two lab locations.

  • What is a "to-do"?

    A to-do is a task that contibutes to progress on a project. Our projects are focused around social good, so your contribution makes an impact. To-dos are usually assigned weekly, and can vary from 3 hours to 40 hours, or anywhere in-between, depending on your level of commitment.

  • Why should I contribute more time?

    Contributing more time in the collaboratory allows us to provide you with a greater variety of skills, understanding, and real-world experience that will enable you to make a larger impact – both on a weekly and long-term basis. This truly is an instance of "you get out of it what you put in."

  • How do you teach the skills necessary to accomplish a "to-do"?

    We use detailed guides that provide background and illustrate in a step-by-step format how to apply different ideas and techniques that will allow you to complete the "to-do".

  • Can I join if I have other commitments, like an internship, job, or school?

    Absolutely! We want to work with you to make this experience great. The minimum commitment is 5 hours, which can usually be fit into an internship, job, or school schedule. We will work with you to find a schedule that is best for everyone.
